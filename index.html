About Us

Crafted with Saudi Soul

Cocora was founded in Riyadh as a Saudi chocolate brand offering a fully integrated chocolate experience — reflecting authentic Saudi taste through premium quality, thoughtful creativity, and meticulous attention to detail.


We select our clients carefully, ensuring every collaboration delivers a premium experience from the very first contact. Every detail — flavor, shape, mold, color, and packaging — is crafted to serve your identity.

Our Vision

The leading Saudi reference in luxury chocolate making

To become the most prominent example of embodying innovation and local identity — achieving a competitive presence aligned with international standards, in harmony with the goals of Saudi Vision 2030.

Our Mission

Luxury chocolate solutions for premium markets

To deliver luxury chocolate solutions that combine global quality standards with local identity, and to design distinctive experiences that meet the expectations of businesses and premium markets.
